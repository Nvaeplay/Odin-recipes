Pasta and tomato

Pasta and tomato sauce create a classic and comforting combination, where al dente noodles perfectly complement the rich, flavorful tomato sauce. The harmony of tender pasta and robust tomato goodness makes for a timeless and satisfying dish.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice (e.g., spaghetti, penne, or rigatoni)
  • water (for boiling pasta)
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (minced, for flavor)
  • Canned curshed tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • Italian season or a combination of dried herbs (such as oregano, basil, and thyme)
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Sugar (optional, to balance acidity in the tomatoes)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for topping)
  • Fresh basil or parsley (optional, for garnish)

Recipe instructions

  1. Boil the Pasta:

    Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

    Pour in the crushed tomatoes or tomato saunce, and season with Italian seasoning or a combination of dried herbs. Stir well and let the sauce simmer for about 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.

  4. Season and Balance:

    Season the sauce with salt and black pepper to taste. If the tomatoes are too acidic, you can add a pinch of sugar to balance the flavors.

  5. Combine Pasta and Sauce

    Add the cooked and drained pasta to the skillet with the tomato sauce. Toss everything together, ensuring the pasta is well-coated in the sauce.

  6. Serve:

    Plate the tomato pasta, and if desired, top with grated Parmesan cheese. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley for added freshness.
